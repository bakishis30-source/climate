About Our Project

This project is aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 13 (Climate Action), focusing on awareness, education, and community engagement to address climate change.

Causes of Climate Change

Greenhouse Gases

Burning fossil fuels increases carbon dioxide and methane in the atmosphere.

Deforestation

Trees absorb CO₂. Cutting them down reduces nature’s ability to balance the climate.

Industrialization

Factories release pollutants and increase energy demand worldwide.

Natural Causes

Volcanic eruptions and ocean currents also play a role in altering global temperatures.

Effects of Climate Change

Rising Temperatures

Global warming is increasing average temperatures, leading to droughts and heatwaves.

Flooding

Melting ice caps and rising seas cause frequent flooding in coastal areas.

Extreme Weather

Storms, hurricanes, and wildfires are becoming more intense and unpredictable.

Impact on Health

Heat stress, malnutrition, and spread of diseases are rising due to climate shifts.

Solutions & Awareness

Renewable Energy

Switching to solar, wind, and hydro reduces greenhouse gas emissions.

Tree Planting

Afforestation projects help restore balance and absorb carbon dioxide.

Community Action

Awareness campaigns, cleanups, and advocacy empower local communities.

Personal Choices

Reduce, reuse, recycle — small lifestyle changes make a big difference.
